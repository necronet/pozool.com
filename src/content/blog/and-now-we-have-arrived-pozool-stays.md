Welcome to Pozool Stays.

We are starting this blog as a place to share what we are building, what we are learning, and why Pozool Stays exists in the first place. This first post is a simple welcome note, but also a way to explain the problem we care about: managing properties is real work, and most of that work happens in the small details.

Pozool Stays is being built for small and medium-sized property managers who live between two worlds. On one side, there is the long-term responsibility of taking care of real estate: knowing what each property is worth, what has been repaired, what furniture or appliances belong to it, what condition it is in, and how much it costs to keep it running. On the other side, there is the daily movement of rentals: reservations, guests, calendars, cleanings, maintenance, payments, and the follow-up that keeps everything from falling through the cracks.

We believe those two worlds should not feel separate. A property is not just a listing, and it is not just an address in a spreadsheet. It is an asset with a history, costs, income, people attached to it, and decisions that need to be made over time. Pozool Stays is our attempt to bring that picture together in one place, so operators can understand both the financial side and the day-to-day operational side without jumping between disconnected tools.

Part of that means helping managers keep better records of each property: purchase information, repairs, furniture, appliances, utilities, taxes, regular maintenance, and other costs that quietly shape the real performance of the asset. Another part means making it easier to remember who helps keep the operation moving, from cleaners and maintenance teams to lawyers, repair companies, and other vendors who become important as the portfolio grows.

The short-term rental side matters just as much. We want Pozool Stays to help with reservations, guests, calendars, and the tasks that happen before, during, and after a stay. At the beginning, we are keeping this practical and grounded. The goal is not to pretend every platform needs to be connected on day one. The goal is to create a clear place where an operator can see what is happening, what needs attention, and what has already been done.

We are also building from our own experience. The phrase "eat your own dog food" is not the prettiest one, but it captures the spirit: we want to use Pozool Stays in the real work ourselves. That means the product has to survive the ordinary days, not just look good in a demo. It has to help when a guest changes plans, when a bill needs to be tracked, when a repair gets delayed, or when someone needs to understand how a property is really performing.

This blog will be where we share that journey. Some posts will be product updates. Some will be thoughts about property operations, hospitality, costs, and lessons we learn along the way. For now, this is simply the starting point. Pozool Stays exists because property operations deserve clarity, and because the people doing the work deserve tools that understand the whole picture.
